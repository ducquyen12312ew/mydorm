\noindent\parbox{\linewidth}{%
Currently, most universities in Vietnam still rely on manual and fragmented procedures for dormitory registration, application review, and accommodation management. Students often have to obtain information through scattered announcements, complete cumbersome administrative procedures, and have little opportunity to visualize their future living environment before making accommodation decisions.

To address these challenges, the E-Dormitory dormitory management system was developed as an integrated web and mobile platform that enables students to access dormitory information in a more intuitive manner through images, 360-degree virtual tours, and 3D room models. These features help students better understand available accommodation options and make informed decisions based on their personal needs and preferences. In addition, the system provides centralized management functions and supports the automation of application review and room allocation processes based on predefined criteria, thereby reducing the workload of administrators and improving operational transparency.

E-Dormitory is built on a multi-tier architecture using modern technologies, including Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB, EJS, Bootstrap, and React Native. The implementation results demonstrate that the system effectively supports dormitory management operations, promotes digital transformation in higher education institutions, and significantly enhances the user experience for both students and administrators.%
